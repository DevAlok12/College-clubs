About

Heritage Institute of Technology (HITK) is a premier engineering institute located in West Bengal. Established in 2001, it has quickly become a hub for aspiring engineers, offering a diverse range of undergraduate and postgraduate programs.

The institute hosts various clubs and organizations that cater to students' interests, ranging from cultural and technical clubs to sports and social service. Each club provides students with a platform to develop their skills, engage with peers, and contribute to the vibrant campus culture.

This page is about the different clubs of Heritage Institute of Technology and their curriculum.

Contacts

Heritage Institute of Technology, popularly known as HITK or HIT is a self-financed institute in the state of West Bengal. It is affiliated with Maulana Abul Kalam Azad University of Technology Kolkata.
