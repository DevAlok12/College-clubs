Welcome to Resonance

Resonance is a music club dedicated to promoting the art of music and performing arts. We organize various events, workshops, and performances that allow students to express their creativity and passion for music.

Join us to experience the joy of music, collaborate with fellow enthusiasts, and be part of an inspiring community!

For more details about our events, you can contact us on our Instagram page.

Heritage Institute of Technology, popularly known as HITK or HIT is a self-financed institute in the state of West Bengal. It is affiliated with Maulana Abul Kalam Azad University of Technology Kolkata.
